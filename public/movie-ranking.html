Great movies in history according to M. Pegini

The top 3 movies that melted my mind

Blade Runner

One of the greatests science fiction movies ever made. In a time where cgi effects were doing its firsts baby steps, Ridley Scott took the bold decicion to make most of the movie with practical effects and history was never the same since then.

Apocalypse Now

The war movie to rule them all, a psychodelic journey into the mouth of darkness and the horrors of war.. oh and it has Marlon Brando too.

Taxi Driver

Another dark journey, but this time into the mind of Travis Bickle a war veteran turned into a taxi driver, disgusted and alienated by society, he bacomes obssessed with helping a young prostitute and all hell brokes loose.
